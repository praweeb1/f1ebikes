The 3000W eBike Kit steps up to a 72V system: a high-power rear hub motor with a matched high-current controller and colour display. It is a noticeable jump over the 2000W, the kind of acceleration that gets a grin every time.

Because it moves real power, it ships with torque arms as standard and runs best on a good-quality 72V pack with the headroom to deliver the current cleanly.

What the kit includes

  • Rear hub motor laced into your chosen wheel (26, 27.5 or 29 inch)
  • Matched sine-wave motor controller
  • Handlebar display (SW900 or UKC1, your choice)
  • Half-twist throttle
  • Pair of brake levers with motor cut-off
  • Pedal-assist (PAS) sensor and magnet disc
  • Pair of torque arms
  • Full wiring harness with waterproof connectors
  • Mounting brackets, spacers and fixings

Specifications

  • 3000W rear hub motor
  • 72V compatible
  • 50A sine-wave controller
  • 100 Nm peak torque
  • 65 km/h top speed (assisted)
  • Fits 26, 27.5 and 29 inch wheels
  • SW900 or UKC1 display
  • Recommended battery: 72V lithium pack, 20Ah or larger

Figures are indicative and vary with rider weight, battery, tyre and terrain. Assisted speeds may exceed road-legal limits, so check your local e-bike laws before riding.

Warranty

Every kit is covered by a 12-month warranty on the motor and controller against manufacturing defects. Wearing parts, crash damage, water ingress from unsealed connectors and damage from over-volting are not covered. Keep your order number and we will sort it out.
