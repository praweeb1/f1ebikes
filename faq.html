Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our conversion kits, batteries, shipping and installation. Can't find your answer? Get in touch.

Do you offer professional installation?

Yes. We're based in Melbourne and professional installation can be arranged for your conversion kit — drop the bike to us and our team will fit and test the whole system for you. Contact us to book a time or get a quote.

Will a kit fit my bike?

Our rear hub-motor kits fit most bikes with standard 135–142mm rear dropouts, and you choose a 26", 27.5" or 29" wheel to match your frame. If you're unsure, send us a photo of your bike through the contact page and we'll confirm the fit.

Is a battery included?

No — the kits are motor kits and don't include a battery. You'll need a compatible lithium pack to match the kit's voltage (48V to 72V depending on the model). Browse our batteries or ask us for a recommendation.

Which kit should I choose?

The 1500W and 2000W kits suit commuting and general riding, while the 3000W and 5000W kits are 72V performance builds for serious speed and torque. Not sure? Tell us how and where you ride via the contact page and we'll point you to the right one.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping is free Australia-wide and typically takes 7 to 14 business days. You'll get tracking as soon as your order leaves our Melbourne warehouse.

What warranty do the kits come with?

Every kit is covered by a 12-month warranty on the motor and controller against manufacturing defects. Wearing parts, crash damage and damage from over-volting aren't covered. Keep your order number and we'll sort out any claim.

Are torque arms included?

Yes. Every conversion kit ships with a pair of torque arms to keep the axle locked safely in the dropouts, which matters most on the higher-power 72V kits.

Can I return a kit?

Unused kits in original packaging can be returned — see our refund policy for the full details, or contact us and we'll help.
